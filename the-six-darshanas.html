The Six Darshanas

Explore the profound wisdom traditions that form the foundation of Indian philosophical thought

Samkhya

The enumeration philosophy exploring the fundamental principles of existence through Purusha and Prakriti.

Yoga

The path of union through disciplined practice, meditation, and the eight-limbed path of Patanjali.

Nyaya

The logic school focusing on valid reasoning, debate, and the systematic analysis of knowledge.

Vaisheshika

The atomistic philosophy examining the fundamental categories and substances of reality.

Mimamsa

The ritual philosophy emphasizing dharma, Vedic interpretation, and the power of sacred action.

Vedanta

The culmination philosophy exploring the ultimate reality of Brahman and the nature of the Self.
